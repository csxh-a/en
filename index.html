About the Company

Footprint

  • In line with the company’s development and the current landscape of the aviation spare parts supply industry, the company introduced a Japanese investor and established a U.S. branch in California in April 2025.
  • In addition to spare parts, we also offer material sales, retrofit solutions, and PMA services.
  • The U.S. branch is mainly responsible for the procurement and distribution of aviation spare parts, handling the repair of high-value components, and the expansion into international markets.
  • The U.S. branch is located in Carlsbad, San Diego, California.
